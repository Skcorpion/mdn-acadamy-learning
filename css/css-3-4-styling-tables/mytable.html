The 12 Verb Tenses
  Past Present Future
Simple I ate pizza yesterday.

S + V2 + O

 I eat pizza everyday.

S + V1 + O

 I will eat pizza tomorrow.

S + 'will' + V + O
Continuous I was eating pizza when you arrived.

S + 'was/were' + (V+ing) + O

 I am eating pizza right now.

S + 'am/is/are' + (V+ing) + O

 I will be eating pizza when you arrive.

S + 'will be' + (V+ing) + O
Prefect I had eaten all of pizza when you arrived.

S + 'had' + V3 + O

 I have eaten all of the pizza.

S + 'have/has' + V3 + O

 I will have eaten all of the pizza by the time you arrive.

S + 'will have' + V3 + O
Prefect Continuous I had been eating pizza for 2 hours when you arrived.

S + 'had been' + (V+ing) + O

 I have been eating pizza for 2 hours.

S + 'had been' + (V+ing) + O

 I will have been eating pizza for 2 hours when you arrive.

S + 'had been' + (V+ing) + O
Legend:   S = Subject      O = Object      V = Verb (V1 = present / V2 = past / V3 = past participle)
